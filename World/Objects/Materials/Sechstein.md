Dark grey stone with a blueish tint, the surface is matte but completely smooth to the touch. [[Sechstein]] tends to form hexagonal columns down to the molecular level and is as hard as diamond. There are no known means of shaping or creating [[Sechstein]].

[[Gateway]]s are build from [[Sechstein]], seemingly causing their hexagonal shape.

"[[Thousndoor]] proper" is located inside a giant hollow [[Sechstein]] column and surrounded by [[The Barrens]], an expanse covered in [[Sechstein]].
