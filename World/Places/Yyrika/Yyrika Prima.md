The central region of [[Yyrika]], once a collection of verdant valleys and alms it was turned into a desolate wasteland by the opening of the [[Immortal Wound]]. Now [[Yyrika Prima]] is primarily inhabited by feral [[Ghoul]]s as well as [[Mist Reavers]] who harvest the never ending streams of [[Necrolithic Mist]] pouring from the [[Immortal Wound]].
