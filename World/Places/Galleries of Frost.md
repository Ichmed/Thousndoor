In the far north of the [[Heavens of the Eye]] there is a seemingly endless expanse of ice. On closer inspection the floor is made of a large collection of frozen bodies from [[Rosmar]], [[Ursarc]] and [[Humans]]. It is tradition among many tribes of the [[Bittermeer]], for shamans near their deathbed to come to this place and join the legions of dead frozen bodies.

[[Shamans]] visit this place and listen to the wind echoing and whispering through the latices of frozen bodies to communicate with their ancestors.
