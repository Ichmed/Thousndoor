A giant expanse of polished Obsidian, broken up by rare groups of [[Sechstein]] columns.
Here the [[Kuoth]] is confined to a comparably narrow channel of [[Sechstein]].

### Climate
During the cold nights rain, snow, and hail fall all over the Mirror. While some of the water drains through the [[Kuoth]] or other small channels, most of the water remains as a thin film on top of the smooth obsidian.
During the day the black ground rapidly heats, causing the water to evaporate into thick haze that quickly over saturates the air. The humid air, combined with the shiny ground causes mirages and spawn numerous [[Aquafabula]]s every day.
