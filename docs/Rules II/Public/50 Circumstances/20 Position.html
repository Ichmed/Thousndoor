20 Position

Each Character can at most have one Position, if they would gain another Position, it replaces the old one.

A position is always an Advantage, having no Position simply means not having an advantageous position compared to other Characters in the same Scene.

Examples

In the Rafters

You have climbed onto a beam high above the rest of the scene. You may attack from up there, granting an additional advantage, or Sacrifice your position by dropping down for a surprise attack.

Hidden in a closet

You have an advantage hiding from pursuers or might have an easier time overhearing a conversation
