Grass Skimmer

Katarmanrans with a canvas of <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Cloudthread.html">Cloudthread</a> as their bows. They are used by the <span class="dead-link">WRK Mongols</span> to traverse the vast reaches of the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Die Sieben Ebenen/Cyclopian Pastures.html">Cyclopian Pastures</a>. While most skimmers are wind powered, those meant for heavy cargo are usually drawn by beasts of burden.
