Grass Skimmer

Katarmanrans with a canvas of Cloudthread as their bows. They are used by the WRK Mongols to traverse the vast reaches of the Cyclopian Pastures. While most skimmers are wind powered, those meant for heavy cargo are usually drawn by beasts of burden.
