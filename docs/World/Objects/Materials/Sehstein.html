Sehstein

Dark grey stone with a blueish tint, the surface is matte but completely smooth to the touch. <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a> tends to form hexagonal columns down to the molecular level and is nearly as hard as diamond. There are no known means of shaping or creating <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a>. <span class="dead-link">Portal</span>s are build from <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a>, seemingly causing their hexagonal shape. "<a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Thousndoor.html">Thousndoor</a> proper" is located inside a giant hollow <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a> column and surrounded by an expanse covered in <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a>.
