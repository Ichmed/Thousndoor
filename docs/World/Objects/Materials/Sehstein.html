Sehstein

Dark grey stone with a blueish tint, the surface is matte but completely smooth to the touch. Sehstein tends to form hexagonal columns down to the molecular level and is nearly as hard as diamond. There are no known means of shaping or creating Sehstein. Portals are build from Sehstein, seemingly causing their hexagonal shape. "Thousndoor proper" is located inside a giant hollow Sehstein column and surrounded by an expanse covered in Sehstein.
