Rods of Renier

A collection of small portals, each about 70cm in length that are small enough to be carried by hand. They have a diameter of 7cm, making them unfeasible for transit or transfer of large goods, they are however incredibly useful for the transportation of light, sound, fluids and small goods. There are currently 4 pairs known to exist:

  1. Location unknown
  2. Used by the Grimor Cereal Corporation to transport grains from Grimor to their mills in the Bay of Imhab
  3. Location unknown
  4. Location unknown
