Morchelheim

[Species:: <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/God Beast.html">God Beast</a>] [Status:: alive]

A 200m tall and 500m long <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/God Beast.html">God Beast</a> that has been overgrown by a <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Manitari.html">Manitari</a> hive. The beast wanders the swamps and steppes of eastern <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Yyrika/Yyrika.html">Yyrika</a>. Mycelium has grown all around and inside of the beast and forms large towers on its back. Wares and visitors to the hive are lowered via platforms on long fungal growths from the top of the beast.
