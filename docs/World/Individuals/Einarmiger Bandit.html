Einarmiger Bandit

[Species:: Human/Ghoul] [Status:: deceased]

Lost his left arm to Archibald earning him his name. After drinking multiple bottles of Vita Life Ultra Pro Extra with Plum flavor the bandit became a Ghoul, grew two additional arms on his right side, and stared directly into the soul stone of Ukutakunaku. The bandit then became obsessed with physically reaching Ukutakunaku, creating a device to pierce the veil and ultimately being consumed by the worm.
