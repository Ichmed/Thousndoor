Einarmiger Bandit

[Species:: Human/<a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Undead/Ghoul.html">Ghoul</a>] [Status:: deceased]

Lost his left arm to <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Individuals/Players/Archibald.html">Archibald</a> earning him his name. After drinking multiple bottles of Vita Life Ultra Pro Extra with Plum flavor the bandit became a <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Undead/Ghoul.html">Ghoul</a>, grew two additional arms on his right side, and stared directly into the soul stone of <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Individuals/Ukutakunaku.html">Ukutakunaku</a>. The bandit then became obsessed with physically reaching <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Individuals/Ukutakunaku.html">Ukutakunaku</a>, creating a device to pierce the <span class="dead-link">veil</span> and ultimately being consumed by the worm.
