Eternal Forest

A landscape filled with canyons and craters filled with <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Necrolithic Mist.html">Necrolithic Mist</a> and overgrown with <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Lickolt.html">Lickolt</a> Trees. The three main valleys are the eyes and mouth of the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Cadaver of Irgos.html">Cadaver of Irgos</a>, who seems to be the source of the necrolithic mist.
