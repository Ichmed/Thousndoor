Bittermeer

A great and rough ocean in the northern reaches of the Heavens of the Eye.

North

The northern half of the Bittermeer contains Brinsprang and terminates at the Folded Fjords

South

The southeastern rim of the Bittermeer where it borders the Cyclopian Pastures is known as the Drowning Shores. North of the Drowning Shores lie the Crawling Banks.
