Bay of Imhab

![[Pasted image 20250122225800.png]] A harsh grassland bordering the sea. Here the Kuoth used to flow into the ocean after winding its way through the Seven Plains. After the opening of the Gate of Gahir the river now flows into Thousndoor instead, where it ends up in the Untermeer. The resulting salination of the ocean has given rise to the regions signature salt ring along the rocky coast.

Geography

A flat steppe surrounded by a semicircle of mountains that is open towards the sea. At the center of the plateau lies a great lake that is fed by the Kuoth and drains into the Gate of Gahir.
