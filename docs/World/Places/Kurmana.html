Kurmana

A large country to the north of Yyrika.

Geography

Most of the country is dominated by temperate rain forest, dotted with tall, shear mountains. ![[Pasted image 20260330232139.png]] The north of the country is dominated by large steppes and deserts

Culture

The culture of local human settlements is heavily influenced by the ruins of an ancient Aigor Shun Realm. Its influence can be found all over Kurmana, from writing, to architecture and religion.
