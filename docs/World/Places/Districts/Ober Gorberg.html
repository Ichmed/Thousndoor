Ober Gorberg

This multiple stories high neighborhood stretches from <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Unter Gorberg.html">Unter Gorberg</a> and <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Yarkut.html">Yarkut</a> in the south, along the city wall up to the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Joharbist.html">Joharbist</a> in the north.

Architecture

The district is dominated by gothic and half timbered buildings that stretch higher the further they are to the north where they finally meet and intermingle with the branches of the Joharbist.

Economics

Most economical activity in Ober Gorberg comes from clerical work such as banking, tax and debt collection as well as <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Undead/Ghoul.html">Ghoul</a> customs management. The <span class="dead-link">Central Ghoul Authority</span>, <span class="dead-link">Office of Mist Quality Assurance</span> and the <span class="dead-link">Ministry for Necronautics</span> as well as <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Vita Life.html">Vita Life</a> are headquartered here.
