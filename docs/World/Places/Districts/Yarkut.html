Yarkut

Named after the original small settlement on the far side of the Portal, Yarkut is now the main portalway connecting <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Yyrika/Yyrika.html">Yyrika</a> and <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Thousndoor.html">Thousndoor</a>.

Architecture

The Portal is fitted with a three level, bidirectional road fashioned from <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Lickolt.html">Lickolt</a> which is used as the main highway for large goods moving from and to <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Yyrika/Yyrika.html">Yyrika</a>. The sides of the Portal are lined with half timbered buildings consisting mostly of taverns, hotels and ghoul stables.
