Yarkut

Named after the original small settlement on the far side of the Portal, Yarkut is now the main portalway connecting Yyrika and Thousndoor.

Architecture

The Portal is fitted with a three level, bidirectional road fashioned from Lickolt which is used as the main highway for large goods moving from and to Yyrika. The sides of the Portal are lined with half timbered buildings consisting mostly of taverns, hotels and ghoul stables.
