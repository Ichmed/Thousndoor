Cadaver of Irgos

An enormous dead <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/God Beast.html">God Beast</a> which floats in the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Mirrored Ocean.html">Mirrored Ocean</a>, with the skull and shoulders penetrating on one side and its hands and feet sticking out on the other.

The oral and ocular cavities form the basins in which the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Eternal Forest.html">Eternal Forest</a> lies. The two hands form the <span class="dead-link">Deadman's Archipelago</span> The left foot is known as <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Paradiso.html">Paradiso</a> The right foot is located under water.
