Blob

Beings dropped from a great height into the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Dead District.html">Dead District</a>. Crushed by the fall they are cursed to continue existing as amorphous masses due to the effects of the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Necrolithic Mist.html">Necrolithic Mist</a>

A lucky few might be collected by <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Occupations/Deathshepperd.html">Deathshepperd</a>s and delivered to the upper city where they can perish.

Being purposely thrown into the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Places/Districts/Dead District.html">Dead District</a> is used as a form of ultimate punishment by certain crime syndicates.
