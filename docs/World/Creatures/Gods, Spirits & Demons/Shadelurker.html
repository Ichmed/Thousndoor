Shadelurker

Eyes that open up on surfaces which are cast in shade. Once a light source hits the wall or a person makes active eye contact with the lurker, the eye will close and immediately vanish. Shadelurkers appear completely on their own and are harmless, however they are often attracted by Remote Viewing.

Because of their association with remote viewing and their general unsettling nature, Shadelurkers are often not welcome and seen as bad omens.
