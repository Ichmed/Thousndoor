Aven

An order of formerly flying birds that have evolved to fill mostly ground dwelling niches. While some species have retained or re-evolved their ability to fly, others, like the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Aven/Basilisks.html">Basilisks</a> have evolved to live as quadrupedal land dwellers.
