Torwacht

A kind of servitor that consists of a torso, a small head, and two big four pointed shields attached to its arms. the two shields can be pressed together to form a hexagonal wall used to block passage through Sehstein tunnels, most importantly Portals.

A skilled Servomancer can open and close the gates if they have access to the Servitor's head.
