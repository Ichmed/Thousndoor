Torwacht

A kind of servitor that consists of a torso, a small head, and two big four pointed shields attached to its arms. the two shields can be pressed together to form a hexagonal wall used to block passage through <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Objects/Materials/Sehstein.html">Sehstein</a> tunnels, most importantly <span class="dead-link">Portal</span>s.

A skilled <span class="dead-link">Servomancer</span> can open and close the gates if they have access to the <a href="/Thousndoor/World/Creatures/Servitors/Servitor.html">Servitor</a>'s head.
