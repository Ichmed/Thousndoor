Everwild

An expansive array of diverse untamed wildernesses filled with spirit animal guides. The geography can include, but is not limited to: jungles, steppes, forests, deserts and tundras. By visiting this Dreamscape often, one can find and befriend a spirit guide which may be summoned to the material world.
